Key Research Focus

I explore how humans interact with robots, with a special interest in social norms, behavior modeling, and the dynamics of trust and communication. My work looks at how these interactions can be simulated and studied in VR/XR environments to better understand real-world human-robot relationships and design more intuitive, socially aware robotic systems.
